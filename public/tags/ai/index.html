Beyond the Bubble: Deconstructing Market Concentration in the AI Era

This report examines the unprecedented market concentration in the S&P 500, driven by a few mega-cap tech stocks in the age of AI. It argues that while valuations are high, this is not a repeat of the dot-com bubble due to the immense profitability and strong fundamentals of today’s market leaders. The analysis covers historical comparisons, the ‘winner-take-all’ economic paradigm, and the primary risks facing investors—not an imminent collapse, but potential long-term stagnation. The report concludes with a strategic playbook, evaluating diversification tactics like equal-weight ETFs (RSP) versus cap-weighted funds (SPY) and the importance of global diversification and active stock selection in the current environment.
